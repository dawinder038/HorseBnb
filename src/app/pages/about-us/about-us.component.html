About US

HorseBnB was created by horse owners for horse owners and
horse enthusiasts. The goal of HorseBnB is to simplify all aspects
of horse ownership. We have seen the need for a platform of this
type for a long time. After years and years of being on the road
with our horses, we realized there has to be a better way to
connect horse people with other horse people.

HorseBnB is the first platform that allows everyone in the horse
world to easily connect with one another. We at HorseBnB want
everyone who has a horse or a barn to have this App on their
phone. We provide solutions for long term
board, short term stays, and horse activities. This is just the beginning for HorseBnB.
We have great plans of expanding HorseBnB to simplify all other
aspects of horse ownership in the future.
